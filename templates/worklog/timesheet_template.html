Records must be maintained in the department for five years. Do not forward to Payroll. Please make any necessary corrections or changes.

Special Note

  1. Time-sheets must be turned in by 5:00 p.m. the day after payday or you may not be paid until the following pay date.
    Please note: This time may change due to Payroll deadlines and University Holidays/closings.
  2. All partial hours worked must be rounded off to the closest quarter hour.
  3. Do not send your time-sheet by courier/campus mail.
  4. Use black or blue ink to complete your time-sheet. Time-sheets in pencil are unacceptable.
  5. Time-sheets must be reviewed and signed by you and your supervisor.
  6. Time-sheets with missing signatures will not be processed. You must submit original time-sheets as copies will not be accepted.
  7. Any and all errors, scratchouts, etc. must be corrected and initialed by both you and your supervisor.
    For example: if you make two errors, both errors must be corrected and initialed by both you and your supervisor.
